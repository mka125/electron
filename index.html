About Naheed Beauty

Empowering women through premium beauty products and exceptional customer service since 1978

Our Story

Founded in 1978, Naheed Beauty began as a small beauty salon with a vision to make premium beauty products accessible to every woman. Over four decades, we have grown into one of the most trusted beauty brands, known for our commitment to quality and innovation.

Our journey started with a simple belief: every woman deserves to feel beautiful and confident. This philosophy continues to guide us as we curate the finest selection of skincare, makeup, fragrances, and hair care products from around the world.

Today, Naheed Beauty serves millions of customers across multiple countries, maintaining the same commitment to excellence that defined our humble beginnings.
